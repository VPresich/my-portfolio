    Valentyna Aleksandrova

    Fullstack Developer

    After completing my studies at Moscow Physics and Technology Institute, I earned a Master's degree in Applied Mathematics and Information Technology. Then I worked as a Researcher at the Institute of Cybernetics, where I completed my postgraduate studies and obtained a Ph.D. (Physics and Mathematics with a specialization in Mathematical Modeling). During this time, I was involved in various research projects at the IC and NTUU KPI. I also engaged in teaching activities as a Senior Lecturer, covering subjects such as Algorithms, Programming Languages, and Relational Databases. I obtained the academic rank of Associate Professor in Information Technology.

    I have extensive experience in developing commercial applications. For the past 8 years I have been working as a C++ developer, continuously striving to enhance and expand my programming skills. In pursuit of this goal, I recently completed a Full Stack Developer course at GoIt, where I participated in team-based Web Application Development projects as a Team Leader.
